FACULTY OF SCIENCE

OBJECTIVES

The Faculty of Science is a vital academic unit within a university dedicated to the pursuit of knowledge and excellence in the field of natural and applied sciences. Comprising various departments and disciplines, the Faculty of Science plays a crucial role in fostering scientific inquiry, innovation, and education.Diverse Disciplines: The Faculty of Science typically encompasses a wide array of disciplines such as Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Computer Science, Environmental Science, and more. This diversity allows students to explore various scientific domains and develop a comprehensive understanding of the natural world.Research and Innovation: Faculty members within the Science Faculty are often engaged in cutting-edge research and contribute to advancements in their respective fields. Research activities may include fundamental scientific inquiries, applied research with real-world implications, and interdisciplinary collaborations.

VISION

The Faculty of Science aspires to be a globally recognized center of excellence in scientific education, research, and innovation. We envision a dynamic and inclusive academic community that cultivates a passion for discovery, fosters interdisciplinary collaboration, and prepares graduates to address the complex challenges of the ever-evolving world. Our commitment is to push the boundaries of scientific knowledge, inspire curiosity, and contribute significantly to the advancement of society through transformative research, innovative education, and meaningful community engagement."It's important to note that each institution may have its unique vision statement tailored to its values, mission, and strategic objectives. The vision statement serves as a guiding force, inspiring faculty members, students, and stakeholders to work towards a shared future that aligns with the principles and goals of the Faculty of Science
