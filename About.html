WELCOME TO THE DEPARTMENT

In the era of Information Technology, centered around Computer Science, the study of this discipline is crucial for society to harness the full benefits of technological advancements and achieve rapid economic progress. Computer Science encompasses the study and development of applications for daily computer use, the supporting software systems, and the ethical means to create reliable and correct systems. Core to Computer Science is the skill of computer programming, and employers seek Computer Scientists proficient in current techniques, tools, and the application of sound engineering principles to program development and maintenance. The department offers a diverse range of courses, equipping students with skills applicable across various economic sectors. Graduates are well-prepared for the computing industry, working collaboratively in project or research teams, bringing specialized skills to the table. The program serves as a foundation for further study or research, exposing students to established and emerging technologies and active research fields in Computer Science. The Department provides two undergraduate degree programs: Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (Single-Major and Major-Minor) and Bachelor of Science in Information Technology.
